 Experience a reimagined automation journey crafted by ChosenArchitect. This pack centers on the mechanical wonders of Create, seamlessly weaving technology and magic into a unified progression. By integrating Just Dire Things, Ars Nouveau, Refined Storage 2, and Replication, this modpack transforms the traditional Create experience into something more accessible and expansive. Whether you are a master engineer or a newcomer to automation, you'll find a curated path that reduces the grind while opening up endless possibilities for innovation and creative design.
